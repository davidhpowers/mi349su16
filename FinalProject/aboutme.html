About Me

Hello, and welcome to my new website.

A little about me, I am from Jackson MI and I attend school at Michigan State University in East Lansing MI. I will be graduating in December 2016 with a degree in Computer Engineering. My goal in life is to use my technical skills to help make the world a better place through the creation of innovative products.

I’ve always had a passion for web development, and I have focused on that in my schooling. I’ve had 3 internships at General Motors, during which I have done SharePoint Development for Global End User Experience and Quality assurance automated testing using selenium for OnStar.

One of my major projects was to create a mobile framework to be deployed across SharePoint sites. This allows the user to create, edit, and view all their SharePoint content on the go, improving user experience and team collaboration.

In addition to development, I also enjoy outdoor activities such as running and biking, and I am an avid science fiction and fantasy reader.

I hope you enjoy the site.

Cheers!
